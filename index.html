Virtual Therapy Activities

Help clients build skills

Mind Play brings engaging, interactive activities into your therapy practice — so clients stay motivated and you always know what's working.

Why it works

🎯

Clients learn by doing

Interactive exercises — breathing techniques, emotion exploration, coping skill quizzes — turn abstract therapy concepts into practiced, repeatable skills.

📊

Therapists see progress

Assign activities, track scores and completion, and walk into every session with a clear picture of where each client is on their journey.

🔄

Growth beyond the session

Assignable activities extend the reach of therapy between appointments, helping clients build habits and momentum on their own schedule.
